About

Where systems
meet soul.

Roma Valentine sits at the intersection of healthcare operations and community organizing — a combination no one else has. With 15+ years in Medicare Risk Adjustment and project management, she brings the precision of clinical systems to the human work of building belonging.

She has coordinated events from intimate community dinners to 300-person multi-day festivals, building every supporting system from the ground up: governance structures, safety protocols, volunteer frameworks, vendor contracts, and harm reduction partnerships.

In 2024, she founded the Kansas City Intentional Communities Leadership Alliance (KC ICLA), convening 20+ leaders from 10+ regional communities into a quarterly collaboration network. She is a certified coder and auditor, a PMP candidate, and a 16-year student of Ayurveda — a practitioner who tracks outcomes to prove the work matters.

Neurodivergent, trauma-informed, and values-driven: Roma doesn't just run events. She builds the infrastructure that turns gatherings into communities.
